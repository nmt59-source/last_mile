Death certificate
Almost every step requires a certified copy: the bank, Social Security, life insurance, the DMV, the probate court, and more. Uploading it here means your name, date, and location fill in automatically across every letter. We also use it to track how many certified copies you have left as you work through the list.
Required
or
Three ways to get it:
1. Funeral home — ask them to order 10 to 15 certified copies. Fastest option.
2. VitalChek.com — official government service, arrives in 5 to 10 business days. Visit VitalChek
3. County vital records office — in person, same day. Bring photo ID and $10 to $25 per copy.
🏦
Bank statement or tax return
This is how we find financial accounts. A bank statement confirms account numbers and institution names. A tax return lists every interest-paying bank, investment account, dividend source, and employer. Together they reveal accounts that might otherwise be missed. When you reach the financial notification tasks, we can scan these to build your complete list automatically.
